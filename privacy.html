Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Our Commitment to You

Welcome to Venu. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about what we collect and how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices for information collected through our website located at venu-app-tech.github.io.

By using our site and joining our waitlist, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate our waitlist and communicate with you:

  • Your email address when you join our waitlist
  • The date and time you submitted your information
  • Basic analytics data such as pages visited and time spent on our site (if analytics are enabled)

We do not collect your name, phone number, payment information, or any other personal details unless you voluntarily provide them by contacting us directly.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To notify you when Venu launches in your city
  • To send you updates about the product and early access opportunities
  • To communicate important announcements related to Venu
  • To understand general interest levels in different cities

We will never use your email for spam, and we will never sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate our site:

  • Formspree — processes waitlist form submissions and stores your email securely
  • GitHub Pages — hosts our website
  • Google Fonts — loads fonts used in our site design

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review their policies if you have concerns.

Data Storage & Security

Your email address is stored securely through Formspree's encrypted servers. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or until you request deletion.

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information. At any time you may:

  • Request to view the personal information we have on file for you
  • Request that we delete your email from our waitlist
  • Opt out of any future communications from us
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us at the email below and we will respond within 7 business days.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We may use essential cookies required for basic site functionality only. We do not use your browsing data for advertising or retargeting purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as Venu grows and evolves. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We're happy to answer any questions you have about how we handle your data.
